LEVELS

Level 0

Players find themselves in a strange room with a box containing a mouse and cheese, introducing them to the quantum world's unpredictable nature. The Schrödinger’s Cat thought experiment is the central theme. Players must understand the concept of quantum superposition, where the cheese is both eaten and not eaten until observed. Correctly understanding this concept allows players to progress.

Level 1

Embrace the principle of superposition by pressing both buttons simultaneously. The action of pressing both buttons unlocks both doors, requiring coordinated use of both hands. The puzzle is designed to symbolize the concept of superposition in quantum physics.

Level 2

Players must manipulate switches in one chamber to affect the other, representing quantum entanglement. The correct switch pattern unlocks the next level.

GAME INFO

Kevin visited the museum located in National and Kapodistrian University of Athens. Curiosity got the best of Kevin, ignoring the sign “Don’t touch the Quantum Computer” and ended up sucked into the quantum world. Targets tech enthusiasts and curious minds aged 16 and up. Immerses players in a virtual quantum world, where mind-bending puzzles await to be solved in order to escape. The core player of the game is Kevin, a curious and quirky student, trapped in the quantum realm.
