PROJECT

IDEA

Quantumscape: Mind-Bending Puzzles and Challenges: Immerses players in a virtual quantum world, where mind-bending puzzles await to be solved in order to escape. Cutting-edge technology, Quantumscape offers players an unparalleled gaming experience.

AESTHETICS

Parallel dimension created by a quantum computer. Features a robust and industrial look. Iron as the primary building material. Design includes intricate ironwork, gears, cogs, and steam pipes. Futuristic and enigmatic atmosphere.

LEVELS

Level 0

In this level, the player embodies a curious student in a Computer Museum, drawn to an exhibit on Quantum Mechanics. Tasked with answering two pivotal questions, their success unveils a forbidden Quantum Computer. Despite clear warnings, their curiosity leads them to touch the device, catapulting them into the Quantum Realm. Here, amidst a world governed by quantum superposition and entanglement, the player must navigate through bewildering puzzles to find a pathway back to reality, all while grappling with the unconventional principles that dictate this extraordinary domain.

Level 1

Embrace the principle of superposition by adding the stones simultaneously. The puzzle is designed to symbolize the concept of superposition in quantum physics.

Level 2

Players must manipulate switches in one chamber to affect the other, representing quantum entanglement. The correct switch pattern unlocks the next level.

GAME INFO

Kevin visited the museum located in National and Kapodistrian University of Athens. Curiosity got the best of Kevin, ignoring the sign “Don’t touch the Quantum Computer” and ended up sucked into the quantum world. Targets tech enthusiasts and curious minds aged 16 and up. Immerses players in a virtual quantum world, where mind-bending puzzles await to be solved in order to escape. The core player of the game is Kevin, a curious and quirky student, trapped in the quantum realm.
